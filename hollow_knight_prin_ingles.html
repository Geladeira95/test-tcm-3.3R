Hollow Knight is a 2D action-adventure game developed and published by Team Cherry. Released in 2017, the title stands out for its charming and dark aesthetic, combined with engaging and challenging gameplay. The game is set in Hallownest, a vast and intricate underground kingdom filled with mysterious creatures and dark secrets. You take on the role of the Knight, a silent and enigmatic hero who explores this decaying world in search of answers about the origin of his own existence and the mysteries surrounding the kingdom.

The mechanics of Hollow Knight are inspired by classics of the "Metroidvania" genre, offering an open world to explore, full of abilities and items to collect. The art is notable for its hand-drawn style, which creates a unique and immersive atmosphere, while the soundtrack is an emotional experience in itself, perfectly complementing the narrative and the game's environment.

Hollow Knight also stands out for its challenging yet rewarding difficulty. The game features complex enemies and powerful bosses, requiring precision and patience. As the Knight progresses, he acquires abilities like the dash and double jump, which not only improve his combat options but also open up new areas for exploration, encouraging the player to revisit old locations with a new perspective. This gradual progression is one of the factors that make the game so satisfying.

The narrative of Hollow Knight is subtly presented, contributing to its mysterious atmosphere. Instead of delivering the story directly, the player gathers information through enigmatic dialogues, item descriptions, and environmental observations.
